    FindIT Square

    Located in the loop in Chicago , our mission at FindIT Square is to enable excellence in the workplace. We tailor solutions across all our offerings to fit your unique need as no two clients are the same. We do not believe in a one size fits all model. We build payment solutions and can also provide you with carefully matched resources for you to manage. Our four sided areas of focus as in the square in our logo represent our strategic expertise in Fintech Domain, Technical Development and analytics expertise, governance and out of the box solutions for the payments industry.

    Services

    Payments

    We offer Payments as a service to enable. Accept a range of transactions from end customers and enable multiple payments methods including credit cards, debit cards and ewallets integrations. Our services include Payment gateways, e-wallets, virtual terminals, merchant self -service website and integrated payments solutions for consolidating within a vertical.

    We offer a roadmap and help small and medium sized businesses implement a modern & scalable solution in the cloud to back office operations and financial business processes. We enable the journey to help you control your business and have clear visibility of financials, supply chain management and billing.
